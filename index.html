Passionate Full Stack Web Developer in the making — I'm a final-year Computer Science & Engineering student at American International University-Bangladesh, focused entirely on the MERN stack (MongoDB, Express.js, React.js, Node.js).

I care about the full journey of a web application: designing a schema that won't fight you later, building REST APIs that are predictable and well-documented, and shipping front-ends that stay responsive and accessible from a phone in Dhaka to a desktop anywhere else.

Across academic projects — an e-commerce platform, a student management system, a standalone authentication service — I've practiced writing clean, maintainable code, wiring up JWT and Firebase authentication, and structuring both relational and non-relational databases. What ties it together is continuous learning: every project is a chance to read one more RFC, refactor one more component, or fix one more edge case.

I'm now looking for a junior full stack role where I can turn that habit of learning into production impact — and keep sharpening my problem-solving instincts alongside engineers who've been doing this longer than I have.
